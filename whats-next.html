What's Next?

SYSTEMS ARE READY

Research and development of WA RUC began in 2012 to find a sustainable, long-term revenue source to replace the gas tax. A decade later, Washington is now ready to begin a transition away from the gas tax to a RUC system.

The Washington State Transportation Commission (WSTC) submitted its report of findings and recommendations [LINK TO LIBRARY SECTION] to the state Legislature in 2020. Legislation was subsequently introduced and continues to be debated. During this time, our research continues, addressing questions from the public and policymakers, including:

THREE KEY FINDINGS HAVE EMERGED SO FAR

Privacy can be protected

RUC harmonizes transportation funding and climate policy objectives

RUC offers a balanced solution that minimizes household transportation costs, especially for low-income and rural drivers
